Help Centre

Find answers to frequently asked questions and get support

What is Nyeri Police and Community Chapel?

Nyeri Police and Community Chapel is a vibrant faith community dedicated to serving the spiritual needs of our church members and the surrounding community. We offer various spiritual resources including Bible study, hymns, prayer support, and community events.

How can I contact the church?

Email: nyeripolice@gmail.com

Phone: Available upon request

Location: Nyeri, Kenya

What are the service times?

Please check the Events page for the latest service times and schedules. You can also contact us directly for more information.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation menu. Fill in your details including name, email, phone number, and password. Choose your role (Church Member or Visitor) and agree to the terms and conditions to create your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, look for a "Forgot Password?" option. Follow the instructions to reset your password using your registered email address.

How do I update my profile?

Log in to your account and go to your profile settings. You can update your name, phone number, and other personal information from there.

Which Bible version is available?

We currently offer the Good News Bible (GNB), which is known for its clear and easy-to-understand translations. Additional versions may be added in the future.

How do I search for specific Bible verses?

Go to the Bible app and use the search function to look up specific verses, chapters, or keywords. You can search by book name, chapter, or verse number.

Can I bookmark verses?

Yes! You can bookmark your favorite verses for quick access. Click the bookmark icon next to any verse to save it to your bookmarks list.

How do I view upcoming events?

Click on the "Events" link in the navigation menu. You'll see all upcoming church events with dates, times, and locations.

How do I register for an event?

On the event details page, look for a registration button or form. Fill in the required information and submit to register for the event.

Can I get reminders for upcoming events?

Yes! You can enable notifications in your account settings to receive reminders for upcoming events.

How do I submit a prayer request?

Go to the "Prayer Requests" page and fill out the form with your name, email (optional), and your prayer request. You can mark it as urgent if needed. Submit to add it to our prayer chain.

Are prayer requests kept confidential?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Prayer requests are reviewed only by authorized church members and are kept confidential.

Can I pray for other people's requests?

Absolutely! We encourage all community members to join our prayer chain. Visit the Prayer Requests page to see requests you can pray for.

What browsers are supported?

Our website works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, please keep your browser updated.

Is the site mobile-friendly?

Yes! The website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

Can I use the site offline?

Yes! We have a Progressive Web App feature that allows you to access certain content offline. Some features like announcements and events may require internet connection.

I'm experiencing issues with the site. What should I do?

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, then reload the page. If the problem persists, contact us at nyeripolice@gmail.com with details about the issue.
